Captain Cool MS Dhoni’s 10 distinct records that make him one of the greatest ever leader

  1. Most successful captain in ICC Championships/trophies

    2. MS Dhoni is the only captain to lead his team (India) to victory in the ICC ODI World Cup in 2011, T20 WorldCup in 2007 and Champions Trophy in 2013. No other captain in the world has the distinction of clinching all three ICC trophies/championships. In his very first assignment as captain, Dhoni won the T20 World Cup in 2007.


  2. Most experienced captain in world cricket

    3. Dhoni has captained most number of international matches (332) — a combination of Test, ODI and T20 — so far in the history of cricket. Australia’s Ricky Ponting (324) is second and New Zealand’s Stephen Fleming (303) comes third in the list.


  3. Century of stumpings

    4. Dhoni is the only wicketkeeper to effect more than 100 stumpings in ODI history (123 in 444 matches). Sri Lankans Kumar Sangakkara (99) and Romesh Kaluwitharana (75) are second and third on the list, respectively.


  4. Century of ODI wins as India skipper

    5. MS Dhoni is the only Indian captain to win more than 100 ODIs (110). The second most successful ODI captain for India is Mohd Azharuddin (90 wins), followed by Sourav Ganguly (76 wins).


  5. Most runs in Test cricket as wicketkeeper-captain

    6. As captain, the right handed Dhoni scored 3,454 runs in 60 Tests. This is the highest among wicketkeeper-captains.


  6. Most catches by an Indian keeper in Tests

    7. Dhoni holds the record for most number of catches (256 in 90 matches) by an Indian wicketkeeper in Test cricket. He is ahead of Syed Kirmani (160 in 88 matches) and current India keeper Rishabh Pant (111 in 31 matches).


  7. Most dismissals in an ODI by an Indian keeper

    8. Dhoni affected six dismissals (five catches & one stumping) vs England at Leeds in 2007. It is the most number of dismissals in one ODI game by an Indian wicketkeeper.


  8. Highest individual score in ODIs by a wicketkeeper

    9. The Ranchi-born Dhoni scored 183 runs against Sri Lanka in 2005. It is the highest individual score by any wicketkeeper in ODI history. South African keeper Quinton de Kock (178) is behind Dhoni, followed by Liton Das (176) of Bangladesh.


  9. Most stumpings in T20I history

    10. Dhoni has effected most number of stumpings (34) in T20I history, ahead of Pakistan’s Kamran Akmal (32), Mushfqur Rahim of Bangladesh (29)


  10. Captaining in most IPL matches

    11. Dhoni has led in the most number of IPL matches (210), for Chennai Super Kings and Rising Pune Supergiant. followed Mumbai Indians’ Rohit Sharma (143) and Virat Kohli (140) of Royals Challengers Bangalore.
